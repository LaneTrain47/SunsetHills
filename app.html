Which buildings have the best view of the sunset?

The fields above represent buildings in a skyline. None of these buildings exceed 10 floors in height. The left-most building will always be able to see the sunset regardless of its height, because its view of the western sky is unobstructed.
But how about the other buildings in the skyline? They will only be able to see the sunset if they are taller than the building(s) preceding them. Insert some sample building heights into these fields to see which buildings will have the best view of the evening sky.
